DESIGN TCF SCHOOL

All TCF Schools are landmarks in their communities. Our school buildings are well-equipped with airy and well lit classrooms, an administrative block, playground, library, computer and science labs to provide students with a stimulating learning environment.
A standard, single unit TCF Primary School has a covered area of approximately 6,500 square feet with 6 classrooms and a capacity of 30 students each, whereas a standard, single unit TCF Secondary School has a covered area of approximately 9,000 square feet with 5 classrooms and a capacity of 36 students each.
